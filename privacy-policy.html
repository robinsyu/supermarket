Introduction

Welcome to Supermarket Simulator ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our game or visit our website.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our game, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our game.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account or profile
  • Save your game progress
  • Contact us with inquiries or feedback
  • Participate in surveys or contests
  • Use our social features

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (if provided)
  • Game preferences and settings

Game Data

We automatically collect certain information about your gameplay, including:

  • Game scores and achievements
  • Level progression and in-game statistics
  • Game preferences and settings
  • Time spent playing
  • Game interactions and feature usage

Technical Information

When you access or use our game, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, model, operating system)
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address
  • Access times and dates
  • Referring website URLs
  • Internet service provider information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  1. Providing and improving our game: To operate, maintain, and enhance your gaming experience, including saving your progress and customizing features.
  2. Communications: To respond to your inquiries, provide support, and send game-related notifications and updates.
  3. Analytics: To understand how players interact with our game, identify areas for improvement, and develop new features.
  4. Security: To protect our game and users from fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  5. Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Third-party service providers: We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our game, conducting business, and providing services to you (such as hosting, analytics, customer service, and marketing).
  • Business transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.
  • Protection of rights: We may share information to protect and defend our rights, property, and the safety of our users or others.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with our game and website. These technologies help us provide a better user experience, analyze usage patterns, and improve our services.

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, some parts of our game may not function properly if you disable cookies.

Analytics Services

We use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to help us understand how players use our game. These services collect information about your use of our game, including frequency of visits, pages visited, and the time spent on our game. We use this information to improve our game and services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, theft, and loss. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our game is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information from our records.

To exercise these rights or to make inquiries about your personal information, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this Privacy Policy.
